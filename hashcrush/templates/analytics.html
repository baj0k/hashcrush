Analytics Definitions

All metrics are scoped by the selected Domain and Hashfile filters.

  • Recovered Accounts counts account records (rows linked through hashfile_hashes). Recovered % = recovered / (recovered + unrecovered) * 100.
  • Recovered Hashes uses unique values: recovered side is distinct recovered plaintexts, unrecovered side is distinct unrecovered ciphertexts.
  • Password Complexity marks a recovered password as meeting complexity when length is at least 8 and at least 3 of 4 character classes exist: lower, upper, digit, symbol.
  • Composition Makeup is derived from recovered passwords only and groups patterns using ?l, ?u, ?d, and ?s. The chart shows the top 4 groups and rolls up the rest into Other.
