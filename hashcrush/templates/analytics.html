Analytics Definitions
  • Recovered Accounts counts scoped account rows linked through hashfile_hashes.
  • Recovered Password Quality is calculated from recovered passwords only. A password meets the current baseline when length is at least 8 and at least 3 of 4 character classes are present: lower, upper, digit, symbol.
  • Password Reuse measures recovered account rows that share the same recovered password within the selected scope.
  • Domain Posture Comparison ranks shared domains using observed weak-password rate, reuse rate, and password-equals-username findings from recovered accounts only.
